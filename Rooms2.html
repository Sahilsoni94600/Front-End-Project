The President Hotels Group takes great pride in ensuring our guests enjoy the most relaxing and comfortable stay. All areas of the resort, from the rooms to the exquisitely landscaped gardens have been totally upgraded and refurbished. As a result, our four hotels conform to, and even exceed, the five-star standard. In addition guests staying at President Hotel are offered a myriad of facilities and activities in which no other hotel in Pattaya can match.

All the rooms and suites have adaptations to all special needs.Door peephole height adjusted for wheelchair users.Lift with Braille signage to all floors.Portable wooden ramps for access to rooms and restaurants.Swimming pools with safety rails.All hotel toilets have disabled facilities.

Our staff will endeavour to assist our guests with special needs as much as they possibly can and will attempt to anticipate their needs, whilst also respecting their independence. If you need anything special, whether it is an aid or a particular diet, please let the staff know and if it is possible, all will be arranged. All of these, combined with special arrangement and decoration of the indoor and outdoor areas, guarantee a comfortable and pleasant accommodation for everyone.
